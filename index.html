How to Play

Click "Brew Coffee!" to make coffees manually. Buy upgrades to automate and boost production!

Items

  • Barista: Passively brews 1 coffee/sec.
  • Coffee Truck: Passively brews 2 coffees/sec.
  • Espresso Machine: Increases coffee per click by 50% each owned.
  • Pour Over Setup: Passively brews 2.5 coffees/sec.
  • Filter Setup: Passively brews 4 coffees/sec.
  • Cold Brew Setup: Passively brews 6 coffees/sec.
