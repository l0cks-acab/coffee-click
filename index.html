How to Play

Click "Brew Coffee!" to make coffees manually. Buy upgrades and items to automate and boost production. Earn Cafe Points by prestiging at 10,000 total coffees — spend them in the Prestige Shop for permanent boosts and automation unlocks. Activate Coffee Rush to temporarily double your coffee production.

Items

  • Barista: Passively brews 1 coffee/sec.
  • Coffee Truck: Passively brews 2 coffees/sec.
  • Espresso Machine: Increases coffee per click by 50% each owned.
  • Pour Over Setup: Passively brews 2.5 coffees/sec.
  • Filter Setup: Passively brews 4 coffees/sec.
  • Cold Brew Setup: Passively brews 6 coffees/sec.
