How to Play

Click "Brew Coffee!" to make coffees manually. Buy upgrades to automate and boost production! Export or import your save data using the controls below.

Items

  • Barista: Automates 1 coffee/sec.
  • Coffee Truck: Automates 2 coffees/sec.
  • Espresso Machine: Increases coffee per click by 50% each owned.
  • Pour Over: Passively brews 2.5 coffees/sec.
  • Filter: Passively brews 4 coffees/sec.
  • Cold Brew: Passively brews 6 coffees/sec.
