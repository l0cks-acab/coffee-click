How to Play

Click "Brew Coffee!" to make coffees manually. Buy upgrades to automate and boost your coffee production!

Items

  • Barista: Automatically brews 1 coffee per second.
  • Coffee Truck: Automates 2 coffees per second.
  • Espresso Machine: Increases coffee per click by 50% each owned.
  • Pour Over Setup: Passively brews 2.5 coffees/sec.
  • Filter Setup: Passively brews 4 coffees/sec.
  • Cold Brew Setup: Passively brews 6 coffees/sec.

Tips

  • Use Coffee Rush boost to temporarily double all production (cooldown 90s).
  • Prestige after 10,000 total coffees to earn Cafe Points for permanent boosts.
